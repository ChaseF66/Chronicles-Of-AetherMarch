## Scene 03 — "What Glitch Remembers"
### Vyx's Workshop | Continuous from Scene 02

## PART ONE: PIP'S STORY

---

[SCENE]
*The workshop. Vyx is sitting on his workbench, Pip across from him. Glitch hovers between them, occasionally drifting toward Vyx and scanning him with that flickering eye. The city outside is doing what it always does — grinding, hissing, the distant sound of Syndicate drones making their sweeps. District 7 never stops making noise.*
*It's almost comforting. Almost.*

[SOUND]
*City ambiance. Steam pipes. Distant machinery. The occasional crackle of Glitch's systems.*

[PIP]
*"Okay. So. You want the long version or the short version?"*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"..."*

[PIP]
*"Long version it is."*

[SCENE]
*She pulls up Glitch's holographic display again — but this time she's not rushing through it. She's taking her time. Like she's been waiting to tell someone this for a long time and she's not going to waste it.*

[PIP]
*"Before the collapse, I was at Ironhaven University. Computer science. Top of my program. Had everything mapped out — internship at a magitek firm, grad school, the whole thing. I was gonna be one of those people, you know? The ones in the upper tiers with the clean air and the good food."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"I believed in Aethon. I mean BELIEVED. Like... they were gonna save the world. Clean energy for everyone, magitek medicine, the whole utopian pitch. I wrote my thesis on their extraction model. Gave them a perfect score."*

*(beat)*

[PIP — quieter]*
*"I was so smart."*

[SCENE]
*Glitch makes a small sound. Almost sympathetic. Pip waves him off.*

[PIP]
*"Then the collapse happened. And I couldn't get home. Quarantine locked everything down overnight — I'm stuck at the university, my family is back in District 7, and nobody is picking up their comms."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"That was two years ago."*

*(beat — long)*

[VYX — quietly]*
*"You haven't heard from them."*

[PIP]
*"I haven't heard from them."*

[SCENE]
*A moment between them. Two people who know exactly what that kind of silence feels like. Glitch drifts toward Vyx again, scans him, drifts away. Neither of them tells him to stop.*

[PIP]
*"So I started digging. Because that's what I do. I needed to understand what happened, who was responsible, what the actual truth was beneath all of Aethon's press releases and municipal reports. And the more I dug..."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"The more I found."*

[SCENE]
*The holographic display shifts — fragmented documents, redacted files, intercepted communications. Six months of obsessive research projected in the air between them. It's overwhelming. It's meticulous. It's the work of someone who had nothing left but answers.*

[PIP]
*"Cover-ups. Falsified environmental reports. The quarantine wasn't about contamination — it was about containment. Keeping people IN so they couldn't talk. Keeping people OUT so they couldn't see."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"And the experiments. God, the experiments."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"They needed biological interface nodes for the Hex Engine to reach full capacity. Real people, augmented, integrated. And they sourced them from the lower districts because..."*

*(beat)*

[PIP — the anger bleeding through for just a second]*
*"Because nobody was going to miss them. Right? That's what the reports say. 'Low social capital. Minimal civic footprint.' Like that means something. Like that makes it okay."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"It doesn't."*

[PIP]
*"No. It doesn't."*

[SCENE]
*She pulls up a specific file. Heavily redacted. More black bars than text. But one name keeps appearing through the redactions, over and over, like the document itself can't quite hide it.*

[PIP]
*"And then there's this."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"This name. It's in everything. Every file I find about the experiment failures, about the classified incidents, about the one subject they couldn't— the one time the process didn't go the way they planned. This name just keeps... appearing."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"Glitch has been cross-referencing it for weeks. Every document, every intercepted signal, every corrupted data fragment we can pull from their network. And every single time..."*

*(beat)*

[SOUND]
*The city outside gets quieter. Not all at once — gradually. Like the world is holding its breath.*

[SCENE]
*Vyx notices it first. That eerie stillness settling over District 7. The grinding stops. The hissing stops. The drones sound far away suddenly.*
*He can hear his own heartbeat.*

[PIP]
*"There was something else too. This morning, in the substation. When that Wraith stopped. When it looked at you."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"Glitch recorded everything. The audio, the signal frequencies, all of it. And when I ran the analysis on what it was trying to say..."*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*Glitch projects a waveform. The distorted, corrupted sound from the substation — three voices layered over each other, broken and wrong. But Glitch has cleaned it. Filtered it. Pulled something recognizable from the noise.*

*Pip looks at Vyx.*

*The city is completely silent now.*

## PART TWO: THE FLASHBACK

---

[SCENE]
*Before she can say it — before the word forms — something pulls Vyx somewhere else entirely.*

*(hard cut to white)*

---

[FLASHBACK]
*Warmth. The kind that doesn't exist anymore.*

[SCENE — FLASHBACK]
*A workshop. But not this one — a real one, clean and full of light. A workbench covered in magitek components, tools hanging in perfect order, the smell of aether oil and soldering metal. This is before. This is when the world still worked.*

*YOUNG VYX — maybe seven or eight — sits on a stool, feet dangling, watching with enormous eyes as YOUNG KAEL — ten, eleven, too serious for his age — carefully lays out the components of a small prototype on the workbench between them.*

*In the background, barely visible for just a moment — their FATHER, DAVAN, passes through the doorway. Smiling at something. A ring catches the light on his hand.*

*He's gone before you can really see him.*

[SOUND]
*Warm. Quiet. The soft hum of aether systems. Two kids in a workshop.*

[YOUNG KAEL]
*"Okay. Pay attention. This part matters."*

[YOUNG VYX]
*"I AM paying attention."*

[YOUNG KAEL]
*"You're looking at the ceiling."*

[YOUNG VYX]
*"I can pay attention AND look at the ceiling."*

[SCENE — FLASHBACK]
*Kael gives him the look. The older brother look. Vyx straightens up immediately.*

[YOUNG KAEL]
*"The aether coil goes here — see how it connects to the relay? If you force it, it cracks. You have to feel when it seats right. There's a click. Small. But you'll know it."*

[YOUNG VYX]
*"How do you know all this stuff?"*

*(beat)*

[YOUNG KAEL]
*"Dad showed me. Now I'm showing you. That's how it works."*

*(beat)*

[YOUNG VYX — quietly, watching his brother's hands]*
*"I wanna know how to do everything you know how to do."*

[SCENE — FLASHBACK]
*Kael looks at him. Really looks at him. And for just a second he's not a kid anymore — he's something older. Something that already knows how much the world is going to ask of both of them.*

[YOUNG KAEL]
*"You will."*

*(beat)*

[YOUNG KAEL]
*"I'll make sure of it."*

---

*(hard cut back)*

## PART THREE: THE WEIGHT

---

[SCENE]
*The workshop. Present. Cold.*

*Vyx's hands are in front of him. He's been staring at them without realizing it. On his right hand — a ring. Simple, worn, clearly old. It catches the dim light of Glitch's display.*

*He closes his hand slowly.*

*(beat — long, held)*

[PIP — very quietly]*
*"Kael."*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*Vyx doesn't move.*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*He doesn't move for a long time.*

*(beat — the longest one)*

[SCENE]
*When he finally speaks, his voice is completely controlled. Which is somehow worse than if it wasn't.*

[VYX]
*"Say that again."*

[PIP]
*"The name in the documents. The subject they couldn't convert. The experiment that failed — that they classified, buried, made disappear — because it was the one time someone didn't break."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"His name is Kael."*

*(beat)*

[VYX — barely audible]*
*"Show me the picture."*

[SCENE]
*Pip hesitates for just a second. Then she pulls it up.*
*The image is corrupted, fragmented — pulled from classified files, partially reconstructed by Glitch. But it's enough.*
*A face. Older than Vyx remembers. Changed in ways that are hard to look at. But unmistakable.*

*(beat — long)*

[VYX]
*"...That's my brother."*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*The silence stretches.*
*Glitch drifts over to Vyx. Scans him. Drifts away. Comes back. Scans him again from a different angle. Pip doesn't stop him this time.*

[VYX — the guilt hitting now, quietly devastating]*
*"Two years. I've been here for two years and he's been..."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"I assumed. I just— I never confirmed anything, I just assumed the worst and I stopped looking and he was out there the whole time trying to—"*

*(beat)*

[VYX — stopping himself]*
*"He hacked into that Wraith. That's what that was. He was trying to reach me."*

*(beat)*

[VYX — something hardening behind his eyes]*
*"He never stopped looking for me."*

*(beat — long)*

[SCENE]
*He looks at his hand. The ring.*
*When he looks up, something has shifted. The grief is still there — it's not going anywhere. But underneath it now, burning through it, is something that looks a lot like purpose.*

[VYX]
*"Where is he."*

[PIP]
*"That's the thing. I don't know exactly. The files have him being transferred — somewhere deeper in Aethon's network. But there's a facility. A processing center about three districts east. That's the last confirmed location before the trail goes cold."*

*(beat)*

[VYX — already standing]*
*"Then that's where we're going."*

[PIP]
*"Okay, hothead — we need a plan first—"*

[VYX]
*"We have a location. We have a direction. That's a plan."*

[PIP]
*"That is the OPPOSITE of a plan, that is a DIRECTION—"*

[SCENE]
*Glitch beeps urgently. Both of them stop.*
*He beeps again. More urgent.*
*Then he projects something new — not Kael's files. Something else. A ping. A signal trace. Coming from inside the workshop.*

*(beat)*

[PIP — going very still]*
*"...Glitch. What is that."*

[SCENE]
*Glitch projects the source of the signal.*
*The aether coil. Still sitting on Vyx's workbench where he left it.*
*Still glowing faintly.*
*Still transmitting.*

---

## PART FOUR: COMPROMISED

---

[VYX]
*"The coil."*

[PIP]
*"Aethon serial number. Every piece of equipment they manufacture has a tracking signature embedded in the—"*

*(beat)*

[PIP — moving fast now]*
*"How long have you had that."*

[VYX]
*"Since this morning. The substation—"*

[PIP]
*"So they've known where we are since this morning."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"...How bad is that."*

[SCENE]
*As if in answer — the sound of boots on the street outside. Coordinated. Multiple sets. Moving fast.*
*Then: a Syndicate frequency on Glitch's scanner. Encrypted, but the signal pattern is unmistakable.*
*Not patrol drones.*
*Response team.*

[PIP — grabbing her gear, already moving]*
*"It's bad. It's very bad. We need to go RIGHT NOW."*

[VYX — grabbing the wrench, then stopping]*
*"The files. Kael's location—"*

[PIP]
*"Glitch has everything. MOVE."*

[SCENE]
*Glitch downloads everything in two seconds flat — every file, every document, every fragment of data about Kael — and kills the holographic display. The workshop goes dark.*
*Outside, the boots stop.*
*A pause.*
*Then the door comes off its hinges.*

*(hard cut)*

## PART FIVE: THE CHASE

---

[SCENE]
*District 7. Pre-dawn. Vyx and Pip running.*

[SOUND]
*Boots on cobblestone. Shouts behind them. The crackle of magitek weapons charging. Glitch weaving between them, projecting a route.*

[PIP — running, barely winded]*
*"Left! Left left left—"*

[VYX — definitely winded]*
*"I KNOW left—"*

[SCENE]
*They cut through an alley, over a collapsed wall, through the skeleton of an abandoned building. Behind them — AETHON RESPONSE AGENTS. Four of them. Full tactical gear, magitek augmented, moving with the kind of precision that means they've done this before.*
*Leading them: COMMANDER SLATER. Tall, masked, deliberate. Not running. Walking. Like he already knows where they're going.*

[PIP]
*"There's a transit junction two blocks north — old line, been dark since the collapse. We can lose them in the maintenance tunnels—"*

[VYX]
*"You know the maintenance tunnels?!"*

[PIP]
*"I know EVERYTHING about this district, keep moving—"*

[SCENE]
*Glitch suddenly cuts left — projecting a warning. One of the agents has gotten ahead of them somehow, cutting off the northern route.*
*They're being herded.*

[VYX]
*"They're not chasing us."*

[PIP]
*"What?"*

[VYX]
*"They're not chasing us — they're HERDING us. They want us to go north."*

*(beat — running)*

[PIP]
*"...So we go south."*

[VYX]
*"We go south."*

[SCENE]
*They cut hard, doubling back through the ruins of what used to be a market square. Overturned stalls, scattered debris, the ghost of a place that used to feed people.*
*The agents adjust. Still coordinated. Still calm.*
*Commander Slater raises a hand.*
*Everything stops.*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*Vyx and Pip skid to a halt.*
*They're in the market square. Agents on three sides.*
*Commander Slater steps forward from the dark.*

[COMMANDER SLATER — calm, almost conversational]*
*"Vyx. You're a hard person to find. Considering you've been in the same district for two years."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"Yeah well. I like it here."*

[COMMANDER SLATER]
*"We know about the files you accessed. We know what you found. And we know you understand what's at stake now."*

*(beat)*

[COMMANDER SLATER]
*"Come with us quietly and this doesn't have to be difficult."*

*(beat)*

[VYX — looking at Pip]*
*"How many?"*

[PIP — quietly, scanning]*
*"Four agents plus him. Glitch is reading two more on the rooftops."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"Right."*

*(beat)*

[VYX — raising the wrench]*
*"It's gonna be difficult."*

*(beat)*

[COMMANDER SLATER — through the pain, almost admiring]*

[VYX]
*"Where is my brother."*

*(beat)*

[COMMANDER SLATER]
*"You'll never reach him."*

[VYX]
*"WHERE. Is my brother."*

*(beat — long)*

[COMMANDER SLATER]
*"...Irongate Processing Center. East corridor, sublevel four. That's where he was. Before they moved him."*

*(beat)*

[COMMANDER SLATER]*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"Irongate."*

*(beat)*

[PIP]
*"Vyx. We need to go. Now."*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*Vyx looks at Sable for a long moment. Something complicated moving behind his eyes.*
*He lowers the wrench.*

[VYX]
*"Don't follow us."*

*(beat)*

[COMMANDER SLATER — quietly]*
*"They'll send more than me next time."*

[VYX]
*"I know."*

## PART SEVEN: THE ESCAPE

---

[SCENE]
*The edge of District 7. The boundary where the quarantine fencing gives way to the outer districts. Vyx and Pip, breathing hard, Glitch hovering between them.*
*Behind them — District 7. Everything Vyx has known for two years. The scavenging routes. Mrs. Orveil's wall. His workshop.*
*Ahead — everything else.*

*(beat)*

[PIP — catching her breath]*
*"Irongate Processing Center. I know where that is. It's a three day journey east. Through some very not great territory."*

*(beat)*

[VYX — looking back at the district one last time]*
*"We have anything we need?"*

[PIP]
*"Glitch has the files. I've got supplies for two days. You've got a wrench."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"That's enough."*

[PIP]
*"It's really not—"*

[VYX]
*"It's enough."*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*Glitch beeps softly. Projects a small map — the route east. Fragmented, incomplete, but pointing in the right direction.*
*Vyx looks at it. Then looks at his hand. The ring.*
*He closes his fist around it.*

[VYX — quietly, to no one in particular]*
*"I'm coming, Kael."*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*They walk forward into the dark.*
*Behind them, District 7 hums and hisses and keeps doing what it's always done.*
*The aether conduits pulse beneath the cobblestones.*
*Somewhere deep in the network, something vast and patient registers their movement.*
*And shifts.*
