# THE PARTY — FINAL VISUAL REFERENCES

## VYX — THE HEART
**Colors:** Blue and toxic green. Equal measure. Always.
**Quote:** *"I fix what's broken. Because someone has to believe it can be fixed."*
**Silhouette:** Lean-athletic. Compressed at rest. Full height in motion.
**Key details:** Long magitek coat, tool loops, wrench, father's ring (warm gold), aether conduit stitching crackling blue-green
**Aura:** Unstable. Alive. Not holy. Not pure. Part of him.
**Energy:** Noctis from the streets. Regal not by birthright — by frequency.

## KAEL — THE MYSTERY
**Colors:** Black and corrupted red. Cold electrical blue bleeding through.
**Quote:** *"I didn't break. That's all. Sometimes that's enough."*
**Silhouette:** Powerful-athletic. Broader than Vyx. Inevitable stillness.
**Key details:** Magitek lattice visible beneath skin of left arm, long dark coat, eyes shift brown → electrical blue
**Aura:** Black with red lightning crackling through it. Storm clouds from inside.
**Energy:** Contained catastrophe. The price of survival is silence.

## PIP — THE SPARK
**Colors:** Neon green and pink. Glitch colors. Holographic chaos.
**Quote:** *"You can't fix stupid. But you can upgrade it."*
**Silhouette:** Compact. Lithe. Always perched somewhere she shouldn't be.
**Key details:** Oversized scavenged jacket, goggles, mismatched gear, Glitch drone at shoulder
**Glitch:** Built. Broken. Rebuilt. Repeatedly. Sarcastic. Loyal. Almost alive. BEEP. BOOP. OBJECTION: EVERYTHING.
**Energy:** In a world that forgot how to dream, she built a future out of sparks.

## RYKER — THE WALL
**Colors:** Grey and black. Amber barely visible. Just buried.
**Quote:** *"I've done things I can't take back. So I do things I can't undo."*
**Silhouette:** 6'4". War-built. Fills a doorway. Gravity itself.
**Key details:** Heavy worn coat, matte black armor, flask (never finished), cracked phone (video on loop), amber detail fading
**Aura:** Doesn't radiate. ABSORBS. A wall people stand behind.
**Energy:** Galuf. A broken protector trying to remember he's still human.
**The phone:** He watches it on loop. He never finishes his drink.
