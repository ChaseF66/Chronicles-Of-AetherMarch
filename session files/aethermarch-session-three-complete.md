## Narrative Framing Document — COMPLETE
**File:** `aethermarch-narrative-framing.md`

### The Single Biggest Decision of Session 3

**Vyx narrates the entire game.**

Not revealed at the end as a twist —
revealed at the beginning as a frame.

Because this story is about inheritance.
The reveal at the start makes every tragedy more tragic.
Every joy more joyful.
The player sits in the room with the child.
Receives the story the same way the child receives it.
That relationship carries the whole game.

### The Frame
- Future. The world is different. Better. Not perfect — never perfect.
- Pip is in the background. At her desk. Still coding. Still building.
- Vyx is older. The ring still on his hand.
- A child or grandchild sits in front of him.
- They've been asking questions about before.

### The Opening
*"I need you to understand something before I tell you any of this."*
*"We weren't heroes."*
*"We were a mechanic who lost his brother.*
*A girl who couldn't leave a stuffed animal behind.*
*And a drone who filed everything because nobody told him not to."*
*"And somehow — that was enough."*
*[The game begins.]*

### The Closing
The child asks: *"Did Glitch really file all of it?"*
Vyx looks at Pip. Pip doesn't look up. But she smiles.
*"Every single entry. From the first slag heap on Day One. To the last."*
*"Can I read it?"*
Vyx looks at the child. Looks at the ring.
*"That's why we called it the Chronicles."*
*[End.]*

### Pip in the Background
She corrects him. Without looking up.
*"That's not how it happened."*
*"That's how I remember it."*
*"You're misremembering."*
*[to the child, unbothered]* *"She does this."*

### Why The Title Is Perfect
The Chronicles of Aethermarch are Glitch's archive.
Not named for a war. Not named for a kingdom. Not named for a hero.
Named for the record.
Named for the archivist.
Named for the idea that someone was paying attention
the whole time even when nobody noticed.

## New Story Elements — LOCKED

### Slab
- Street name for contaminated aether runoff
- Vyx's form: dark residue balm in a small dented tin
- Origin: leaked from his workshop ceiling in District 7
- He collected it thinking it was machine oil runoff
- Got it on his arms one day. Took the edge off immediately.
- Has used it every night since. Didn't know what it was called.
- Pip recognizes it immediately. "Vyx... is that Slab?"
- Vyx: "It's my balm. It helps me sleep."
- This is the funniest and most human thing about him.

### Wyrm
- Stuffed aetherwyrm. Faded blue-green. Four stubby wings.
- Two button eyes, one more askew than the other.
- Won by Pip's dad at the District 4 fair. Four years ago.
- He missed six times. Booth guy handed it over out of sympathy.
- Dad held it up like he'd won something real.
- Pip almost left it when she packed for university.
- She couldn't.
- It's all she has left of before.
- Glitch has been scanning it since the day Pip built him.
- UNKNOWN ENTITY. Every single time.
- Until Pip says the name out loud to Vyx.
- Two years of open tickets. Finally closed.

### The Aether-Corrupted Maintenance Drone
- First bestiary entry. First combat on the road east.
- MC-class pipeline maintenance unit.
- Two years of raw aether bleed saturation.
- Makes a sound: not mechanical, not a growl —
  an aether-ridden warble. Something that has no name yet.
- Not malicious. Confused. Determined.
- Shut down, not killed.
- "It just wanted to do its job."
- Filed by Glitch under: NOTED.

### The Aether Crystal Formations
- Processed aether byproduct saturated by pipeline bleed over two years.
- Molecular structure rebuilds around aether frequency.
- Grows in branching formations through the slag heaps.
- Blue-green. Luminous. Almost biological.
- Pulses faintly. Like a heartbeat.
- Responds to proximity — especially Vyx's.
- Not technically alive.
- Kind of. Yeah.
- First new natural phenomenon of the road east.
- Glitch files seven formation subtypes. Type A through G.

### The MC-7 Crawler
- Maintenance crawler. Still running.
- Assigned to a pipeline section that collapsed two years ago.
- Treads moving against nothing.
- Arms extending and retracting.
- Status light: green. System nominal. Task in progress.
- 730 days since the task became impossible.
- Still trying.
- Filed by Glitch: NOTED: IT IS STILL TRYING.
- Vyx stands there for a long time.
