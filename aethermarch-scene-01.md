# CHRONICLES OF AETHERMARCH
## Scene 01 — "Something In The Wreckage"
### Lower District 7 | Pre-dawn

---

> **SCRIPT FORMAT GUIDE**
> `[SCENE]` — what the player sees on screen
> `[SOUND]` — audio/ambient description
> `[VYX]` — Vyx speaking (dialogue box, portrait left)
> `[NARRATION]` — text on screen, no portrait
> `(beat)` — a pause, a moment, let it breathe

---

---

## PART ONE: THE CITY BEFORE THE WORLD WAKES

---

[SCENE]
*Black screen. Slow fade in.*
*The city of Aethermarch from above — a towering, industrial sprawl swallowed in pre-dawn fog. Steam vents exhale white clouds between ancient stone towers. Aether conduits pulse faint blue beneath the cobblestones, the city's veins still beating.*
*The upper tiers glow warmly. Down below, in the lower districts, everything is the color of rust and shadow.*

[SOUND]
*Low ambient hum of the city. Distant pipe pressure. The occasional clank of cooling metal.*

[NARRATION]
*The city of Aethermarch never fully sleeps.*
*It just gets quieter about what it's doing.*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*Camera drifts downward — past the gleaming corporate towers of the upper tiers, past the mid-city transit lines gone dark for the night, down into the smoke and tangle of Lower District 7.*
*A quarantine notice is plastered to a crumbling wall: "AETHERIC CONTAMINATION — DISTRICT 7 RESTRICTED BY ORDER OF AETHON SYNDICATE MUNICIPAL AUTHORITY."*
*Someone has drawn a wrench through the official seal. Underneath, in spray-painted letters: "WE STILL LIVE HERE."*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*Camera finds VYX — small against the wreckage of a collapsed aetheric substation. He moves through the rubble with practiced ease, boots careful on the unstable ground, bag over one shoulder. He's wearing a patched-up work jacket covered in tool loops, most of them full. A wrench hangs at his belt like a weapon.*
*He looks young. He moves like someone older.*

[SOUND]
*Debris shifting underfoot. Hissing steam from a cracked pipe nearby. Vyx humming something tuneless.*

---

## PART TWO: JUST ANOTHER MORNING

---

[VYX]
*"Okay. Collapsed junction box — that's copper wire, that's mine."*

[SCENE]
*He crouches, pulls a coil of wire free, examines it, stuffs it in the bag.*

[VYX]
*"Cracked aether housing — worthless, nobody buys cracked. Unless..."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"...No, it's just cracked. Moving on."*

[SCENE]
*He picks his way deeper into the ruin. The substation collapsed maybe a week ago — the Syndicate's official report called it "structural failure due to aetheric resonance overload." Nobody in District 7 believed that. Nobody said so out loud.*

[VYX]
*"Three more of these this month. Three."*
*"Syndicate says it's contamination. Sure. The pipes just started exploding themselves. Very natural. Very normal city behavior."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"Dad would've known what was actually happening."*

*(beat — longer this time)*

[VYX]
*"...Whatever. Eyes open, Vyx."*

[SCENE]
*He shakes it off. Keeps moving. His boot catches on something half-buried — he pulls it free.*
*It's an aether coil. Still intact. Still faintly glowing.*
*He stares at it.*

[VYX]
*"...No."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"Yes."*

*(beat)*

[VYX — quietly, like he's afraid to jinx it]*
*"That's rent. That's actually rent."*

[SCENE]
*He grins — and for just a second, he looks exactly his age. He drops it carefully into the bag. Pats the bag. Looks up at the pre-dawn sky through the collapsed roof.*

[VYX]
*"Okay. One more sweep and I'm out. Don't get greedy, don't get—"*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*He stops.*
*Something in the rubble ahead of him is moving.*

---

## PART THREE: THE THING IN THE DARK

---

[SOUND]
*The ambient hum of the city cuts out. Sudden, total quiet — like something has swallowed the sound.*
*Then: a low, arrhythmic clicking. Metal on stone. Getting closer.*

[SCENE]
*Vyx goes very still. His hand moves slowly to the wrench at his belt.*

[VYX — barely a whisper]*
*"...Okay. Okay okay okay."*

[SCENE]
*The debris shifts. A shape rises from the rubble.*
*It is wrong in the way they are all wrong — too many joints bending in too many directions, a body that was once human stretched and rebuilt into something the Hex Engine found more useful. Magitek lattice visible beneath pale skin. Eyes that glow a cold, dead blue.*
*A HEX WRAITH. The monsters the Syndicate blames on "aetheric contamination." The things that have been appearing in the lower districts for months, driving people out, justifying the quarantine.*
*It is between Vyx and the only exit.*

[VYX]
*"Right. Great. Perfect start to the morning."*

[SCENE]
*He raises the wrench. The Wraith orients toward him — that horrible, mechanical head-tilt, sensors sweeping.*
*It takes one step forward.*
*Vyx braces.*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*The Wraith stops.*

*(beat)*

[SOUND]
*A sound unlike anything else — the Wraith's signal, that constant crackling static that surrounds them, stutters. Skips. Like a record scratching.*

[SCENE]
*The blue light in its eyes flickers. Once. Twice.*
*It tilts its head the other direction.*
*It takes one step — not toward the exit. Toward Vyx. But not aggressive. Something else entirely.*
*Like it recognizes him.*

*(beat — long, held)*

[VYX — very quietly, not moving a muscle]*
*"...What are you doing?"*

[SCENE]
*The Wraith makes a sound. Not a screech. Not the mechanical howl they usually make. Something distorted, broken, layered — like three voices trying to speak through the same damaged speaker.*
*It sounds almost like a name.*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*Then the moment shatters.*
*Something in the distance — a Syndicate patrol drone, sweeping its light through the district. The Wraith's head snaps toward it. Its signal spikes back to full intensity. The recognition, whatever it was, is gone.*
*It lurches sideways, movements suddenly violent and erratic, and vanishes into the dark of the lower ruins.*

[SOUND]
*The city's ambient hum returns. Like nothing happened.*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*Vyx stands alone in the wreckage. Wrench still raised. Aether coil forgotten in his bag. He stares at the dark where the Wraith disappeared.*

*(beat — long)*

[VYX]
*"...Okay."*

*(beat)*

[VYX]
*"That was new."*

*(beat)*

[SCENE]
*He slowly lowers the wrench. Looks at his hand — it's not shaking, which surprises him.*
*He looks back at the dark one more time.*
*Then he turns and walks out of the ruin, into the pre-dawn streets of Lower District 7.*
*Above him, the city hums. The aether conduits pulse.*
*Somewhere deep beneath the cobblestones, something vast and patient pulses back.*

## PART FOUR: TITLE CARD

---

[SCENE]
*Black screen.*

[SOUND]
*A single, low mechanical tone. Held.*

[SCENE]
*White text appears, one word at a time:*

**CHRONICLES**
**OF**
**AETHERMARCH**

*(beat)*

*Beneath it, smaller:*
*"A city runs on power. Power runs on something else."*
