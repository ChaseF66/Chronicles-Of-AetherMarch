## System Document — Aether, Jobs & The Fingulin Bloodline

# RAW AETHER — THE SOURCE

Aether is not technology. It never was.

Aether is the living energy of Aethermarch itself — drawn from deep geological veins beneath the city, older than any structure built above them. Natural. Organic. The closest thing this world has to what other civilizations might call magic. It flows through the earth the way blood flows through a body. It connects everything. It remembers everything.

Aethon didn't create aether. They learned to extract it. Process it. Control it. Build technology around it. They positioned themselves as the necessary middle man between humanity and the most powerful natural force on the planet — and then they made sure nobody could access it without going through them.

That was the business model. That was the empire.

**Raw aether** — unprocessed, uncontained, wild — still exists. It bleeds from degraded pipeline seals on the road east. It crystallizes in the processed byproduct slag heaps of the industrial corridor. It saturates the air of District 7 in corrupted form. It flows in the deep veins beneath the cobblestones, untouched by Aethon's extraction systems.

Raw aether is illegal to use. It belongs to Aethon by municipal decree. Every drop of it, technically, is their property.

The people of the lower districts use it anyway. Black market aether. Siphoned from pipelines, crystallized from byproduct, drawn from the saturated air. Dangerous. Toxic if mishandled. Powerful if you know what you're doing.

Using raw aether isn't discovering a power system. It's POACHING from the machine that's consuming the world. That distinction matters — thematically and legally.

Pip knows what she's doing.

# THE COLLAPSE — WHAT ACTUALLY HAPPENED

Before the collapse, Aethon was working on something that would have changed everything: a way to give ordinary people access to processed aether magic. Not through cybernetics. Not through expensive technology. A portable, accessible system that would allow anyone to touch magic in their daily life.

Technology plus magic. The ultimate utopian promise.

They called the project **the Attunement Initiative.**

Something went wrong. The prototype failed. Catastrophically. The attempt to bridge processed aether with human biology on a mass scale destabilized the extraction network, overwhelmed the Hex Engine's processing capacity, and triggered the cascade failure that became the collapse.

Aethon buried this. Classified everything. Blamed aetheric contamination, structural failure, anything but the truth.

The truth: **they caused the collapse trying to give people power.** Then they retreated to the upper districts, maintained their own access to that power, and left everyone below to deal with the consequences.

The Attunement Initiative files are the most classified documents in Aethon's system. Pip has fragments. Just fragments. Enough to know the project existed. Not enough to know what it was really attempting.

Not yet.

# NATURAL ATTUNEMENT — WHO CAN USE RAW AETHER

Not everyone can touch raw aether safely. Most people who try end up with aether sickness at best, catastrophic system failure at worst. The processed versions Aethon sells are safer precisely because they engineered them to be compatible with average human biology.

But some people are naturally attuned.

Two pathways to natural attunement:

## Exposure Attunement
Years of living in aether-saturated environments. The lower districts — especially District 7 — have been bleeding raw aether into the air since the collapse. People who stayed absorbed it slowly. It seeped into them. Changed their biology at a fundamental level.

Not everyone who stayed is attuned. It requires a baseline compatibility that most people don't have. But those who do, who stayed long enough, who absorbed enough — they can touch raw aether without dying.

Vyx has been breathing District 7's air for two years. Among other things.

## Frequency Attunement
Some people are born with a natural resonance with specific aether frequencies. Not trained. Not developed. Intrinsic. These people don't just tolerate raw aether — they speak its language. They can shape it, direct it, amplify it without processing.

This is rarer than exposure attunement. Orders of magnitude more powerful.

This is the basis of the job system.

# THE JOB SYSTEM — FREQUENCIES, NOT CLASSES

The party's jobs are not trained skills. They are not classes assigned by a guild or a system or a crystal. They are **frequencies** — the specific aether resonance each person naturally aligns with.

Vyx doesn't fight like a Machinist because he trained as one. He fights like a Machinist because that's the frequency his body naturally produces. The gadgets, the tools, the mechanical intuition — these aren't skills he learned. They're expressions of his resonance made physical.

Pip doesn't hack and steal and move like a ghost because she practiced. She does it because that's how her frequency expresses itself — quick, adaptive, finding the gaps in systems the way water finds cracks in stone.

**The frequency IS the person.** The job is just the name they give it.

## Why Some People Can Change Jobs
Natural frequency attunement has flexibility for most people — their resonance can shift, can be trained into adjacent frequencies, can expand through experience and intention. This is what job changing represents mechanically. Not retraining. **Re-attuning.**

## Why Kael Cannot Change Jobs
His frequency was forced through Hextech augmentation. The process locked his resonance at the Hexblade frequency — cybernetic, corrupted, the specific signature of someone whose natural power fought against technological override and won a compromise. He cannot re-attune because his attunement is no longer purely natural. It is fused with the technology.

Permanent. Until his arc resolves.

## When Kael's Arc Resolves
His frequency doesn't unlock like a gate opening. It **EVOLVES.**

The technology he has been assimilating, the cybernetics his resonance has been consuming and making his own — they become something new. Something that isn't Aethon's anymore. His final form isn't a return to who he was before the experiments. It's the completion of who he's been becoming through them.

That distinction matters enormously to Kael. And to Vyx.

# THE FINGULIN BLOODLINE — THE RESONANCE THAT SCARED AETHON

## What It Is
Davan Fingulin was an Aethon engineer. A brilliant one. The kind of mind that understood aether at a level most of his colleagues couldn't reach.

What Aethon didn't fully understand when they hired him — what they only began to suspect after years of working with him — was WHY he understood it so intuitively.

The Fingulin family carries a hereditary aether frequency unlike anything in Aethon's catalogued records. Not just strong. Not just flexible. **Fundamentally different in kind.**

Where most natural attunement aligns with specific processed aether frequencies — the frequencies Aethon built their entire technological system around — the Fingulin resonance aligns with something deeper. Something pre-processing. Something that speaks to raw aether at the source level, at the geological vein level, at the level of what aether actually IS before Aethon touches it.

They don't access aether through Aethon's system. They bypass it entirely.

## Why Aethon Is Afraid
Everything Aethon built — the processing infrastructure, the Hex Engine, the cybernetics, the entire technological empire — is built on one assumption:

**Raw aether requires mediation. It cannot be accessed directly. Human biology is incompatible with it without processing.**

The Fingulin bloodline disproves this.

If one family can access raw aether directly — if that ability can be understood, documented, shared — then Aethon's mediation becomes optional. Their infrastructure becomes a choice rather than a necessity. Their entire power structure becomes negotiable.

They cannot allow this to be known.

They cannot allow this bloodline to survive.

## What Davan Discovered
Working inside Aethon, Davan began to understand what his own frequency meant. He started to piece together what the Attunement Initiative was really attempting — not to give people access to processed magic, but to artificially replicate the natural attunement his family carried. They were trying to manufacture what he was born with.

He started documenting this. Making connections. Building a case.

He disappeared before he could go public.

His fate is unknown. This will not remain the case.

## What Happened to Kael
When Aethon identified the Fingulin bloodline as a threat, Kael became their primary research subject. The goal: understand the frequency. Replicate it. Control it. Weaponize it.

What they got instead: a subject whose frequency was so strong it rejected every override they attempted.

The Hex Engine's signal couldn't overwrite it. The cybernetic integration couldn't contain it. His natural resonance didn't just resist — it began **consuming** the technology. Assimilating it. Turning Aethon's tools into extensions of his own power.

He is not half-human, half-machine in conflict. He is a human whose natural magic is slowly making the machine its own.

**That's why Ryker Sable couldn't break him.**

It wasn't just Kael's words. His honesty. His refusal to submit. It was his frequency — present in the room, undeniable, the specific resonance of someone whose power exists below the level of conscious thought. Ryker felt it without understanding what he was feeling. It paralyzed something in him. The words then finished what the frequency started.

**That's how he hacks into the Wraith to reach Vyx.**

It's not technical skill. It's resonance. He speaks the Hex Engine's language — not because he learned it, but because his frequency is so fundamental that the Engine can't fully distinguish it from its own signal. He's inside the system because the system can't tell him apart from itself.

## What Vyx Carries
The same frequency. Undeveloped. Unawakened.

Two years of District 7's aether saturation have primed him — his biology absorbing raw aether, building tolerance, building the pathways that will eventually allow him to access what he was born to access. The exposure attunement and the frequency attunement working together, amplifying each other.

He doesn't know any of this yet.

When Pip shows him how to use raw aether on the road east — it's not teaching him something new. It's striking a match near something that has been waiting to catch for twenty-two years.

**Why the Wraith stopped in Scene 01:**
His frequency. Present in the space between them. The Hex Engine's signal — which controls the Wraith — registering something it recognized. Something from Kael. Something from the bloodline. Something it couldn't process as a threat because it was too close to its own source frequency.

It hesitated because the aether didn't know what to do with him.

It won't hesitate forever.

## Vyx's Ultimate Class — The Revelation
Unknown. To the player. To Vyx. To everyone except possibly Davan — and Davan is gone.

Pieces will surface as the story progresses:
- Moments where his aether use behaves differently than it should
- Glitch registering anomalous frequency readings that he can't explain
- Enemies hesitating before attacking him
- The Hex Engine's behavior changing when he's near it
- Kael recognizing something in his brother that he can't name

The reveal, when it comes, will recontextualize everything that came before it.

What we know about it:
- It is the completion of the Fingulin bloodline's natural evolution
- It is what Aethon tried to manufacture with the Attunement Initiative and failed
- It is what they have been trying to eliminate ever since
- It is, in the end, the answer to what raw aether actually is — and what it's been trying to do all along

The name of this class is not documented here. Not yet. Some things need to be discovered, not written down in advance.

# THE AETHER SYSTEM IN GAMEPLAY

## How Raw Aether Works
Raw aether is gathered from the environment — crystallized byproduct, pipeline bleeds, saturated zones. It functions as a consumable resource distinct from MP. Where MP represents the character's trained magical capacity, raw aether represents direct access to the source.

**Raw aether can:**
- Enhance existing skills beyond their normal parameters
- Unlock temporary abilities unavailable through the standard skill system
- Interact with the environment in ways processed aether cannot
- Affect Hex Engine-connected enemies differently than processed attacks

**Raw aether is dangerous:**
- Unstable in large quantities
- Corrupted raw aether from District 7's contaminated zone causes debuffs if used carelessly
- Different characters have different tolerance levels based on their attunement

## Pip Teaching Vyx — The Road East Scene
The moment on the road east where Pip shows Vyx how to use raw aether is the game's introduction to the system. It plays as a tutorial but feels like a character moment — because it is.

Pip is self-taught. She found references to raw aether use on the dark web — pre-collapse classified research documents, fragments of the Attunement Initiative's theoretical framework, underground guides written by lower-district practitioners who figured it out through trial and error.

She treats it as chemistry. As something to be understood technically.

Vyx treats it as instinct. He doesn't understand WHY it works the way it does for him. It just does. More easily than it should. More powerfully than it should.

Pip notices.

Doesn't say anything yet.

But she notices.
